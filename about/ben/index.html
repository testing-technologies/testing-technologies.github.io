Benjamin M. Hart

Senior Scientist

Ben is no stranger to drug testing. He worked several years for Keystone Labratories, Inc. doing forensic drug testing. He was the Director of Technology and Certifying Scientist. Ben was in charge of their forensic drug testing lab. He was the chief of personnel, and directed all the new instrument purchases.

His skills for instrument maintenance, reviewing and certifying results, and method development made him the ideal candidate to become our Senior Scientist.

Ben is skilled at developing and validating LC/MS, GC/FID methods and is our chief in charge of devloping our sweeping new Pesticide testing protocols. Ben has been the principal developer of our new Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) software, which will help us serve your needs better and put information you want and need at our fingertips.

Ben is also a teacher. He has been responsible for training and certifying our technicians. He has a history of teaching: While at WCU Ben taught 3 lab classes per semester teaching students the complex concepts of chemistry and the safety standards required for working in a volitile industry. He was a Dean’s List scholar and has the work ethic that all of us in this industry need to be succesful.
